ELEVATOR ALGORITHM

Know which floor number you intend to visit;
Enter elevator;
	Clear doorway;
	Doors will shut;
View wall panel with numbers next to elevator door; 
	Press the button with the number that corresponds with the floor number you wish 	to visit;
	Button will light up;
	You will feel elevator move;
		If not, press button again until it lights up and you feel elevator 			moving;
You will feel elevator stop;
	Button light will go off;
		Confirm it is your number floor light that goes off;
	Doors will open;
		Check side of open door where floor number is often written;
	If all is true, exit elevator.
	If not, press button and repeat steps from last time you pressed the button.

CROSSWALK WITH CROSSLIGHT ALGORITHM

Wait safely at curb facing other side of street you intend to cross over to;
Notice the white crosswalk lines painted on the street outlining the path you will take;
Confirm crosswalk light is red;
	There is often a secondary light shaped like hand in stop position lit red;
	If light is not red but green, move ahead one step in process;
Push button on pole;
	Button is often yellow;
Wait for red light to switch to green color;
	There is often a secondary light shaped like walking person lit white;
Look left and right to confirm traffic has stopped;
	If traffic has stopped, proceed with caution to other side of street prior to 		light returning to red color;
	If not, wait until traffic has stopped and light is green to proceed across.
